Webinar Series Attendance Overview

Dashboard using Power BI

This Power BI project tells a story about webinar performance and its impact on high-level decision-making. As a powerful visualization tool, Power BI allowed me to transform raw data into actionable insights, despite its integration limitations on the web.

  • Webinar Attendees: Analyzing attendance trends to understand participant engagement, identify popular sessions, and reveal areas for improvement.
  • Net Promoter Score (NPS): Visualizing customer feedback to measure satisfaction levels and pinpoint factors influencing overall sentiment.
  • Customer Location: Mapping audience demographics to highlight geographic concentration and uncover untapped regions.
  • Target Audience (C-Suite): Tailoring insights to resonate with decision-makers, ensuring dashboards address their strategic priorities and align with business goals.

The process began with meticulous data cleaning and preparation, where I integrated multiple datasets into a unified source of truth. Using Power BI, I connected these datasets, created meaningful relationships, and built dashboards that balanced design and functionality. This included interactive filters, detailed drilldowns, and clear visualizations such as bar charts, line graphs, and heatmaps.

Each dashboard was designed with the end user in mind, delivering insights that empower stakeholders to make data-driven decisions. This project showcases my expertise in Power BI, from data preparation to crafting impactful visual narratives, and demonstrates my ability to create solutions that inform and inspire action.
